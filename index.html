Experience

Internship at Pusat Islam, UiTM Cawangan Negeri Sembilan Kampus Rembau at Unit Hal Ehwal Islam(UHEI)- UHEI is a department within UiTM that is responsible for managing Islamic affairs at the university. The function of UHEI is administrative affairs, sharing value and community service, guidance and human development, mosque management and enrichment, management of zakat, donations, and endowments. Services at UHEI is religious help and advice for a group, spiritual activities curriculum, managing spiritual and community programs. The mission of UHEI is to encourage learning and thinking through different spiritual activities and the vision is to encourage a culture of learning and understanding through spiritual activities. A public relations, talent and event management company.
